My Services

Programmer

I have come to study the higher cycle of ARI (Industrial Automation and Robotics) because in the middle cycle of Electrical and Automatic Installations we touched on programming a little, thus realizing that I liked it a lot.

And in turn, I arrived the middle cycle thanks to the fact that I did the technology elective in ESO, studying the basics of electricity and seeing that I could be good at it.

Graphic Design

In graphic design, I realized that I liked to draw and design when I made a drawing for my grandfather, which although it was simple, I saw that it was quite entertaining. Later I began to enter the world of layout, developing more graphic design.

I have set myself the goal of designing and creating characters and landscapes through applications such as Blender, Unreal Engine...

Content Creator

I started making videos because in an ARI module they ask for a video of a robot working, and when I started doing it I thought: "Why not do the whole process and upload it to YouTube in case someone needs to know how it's done".

From that moment I have started to make, edit and upload videos to document everything I am doing and if at any time I need to remember something, I will have it.
